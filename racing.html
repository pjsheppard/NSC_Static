Club Racing

Club racing takes place Saturdays and Sundays as well as Thursday evenings from May to late August. A variety of dinghy classes are sailed at the Club, particularly Laser, Albacore, GP14, Enterprise, Solo and National 12.

See Calendar for a full list of events.

Races are sailed under the current Racing Rules of Sailing (RRS) laid down by International Sailing Federation (ISAF) and the requirements of the Club's Sailing Instructions.
